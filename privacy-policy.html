Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 21, 2026

At EarnSphere, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use EarnSphere:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile details when you register.
  • Activity Data: Tasks you complete, surveys you take, rewards you earn, and your wallet balance.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, and device type for analytics and security.
  • Uploaded Content: Images you upload as part of tasks (these are stored securely).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our earning platform and features.
  • Process and credit rewards for completed tasks and surveys.
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, and support.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure the security of our platform.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud) with industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase – for authentication, database, and storage.
  • Google AdSense – to display relevant ads (you can opt out via Google's ad settings).
  • EmailJS – for sending support and notification emails.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience, remember your preferences, and serve relevant ads. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.
  • Request a copy of the data we hold about you.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@earnspherehub.name.ng.

7. Children's Privacy

EarnSphere is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@earnspherehub.name.ng
  • Website: earnspherehub.name.ng
