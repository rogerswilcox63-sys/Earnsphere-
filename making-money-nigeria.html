How EarnSphere Rewards Work: Tasks & Withdrawals Guide 2026

June 25, 2026 | EarnSphere Team

Welcome to EarnSphere. This guide explains how our platform works, the types of tasks available on your dashboard, how points convert to Naira, and how to withdraw your balance to your Nigerian bank account.

Important: EarnSphere is a rewards platform, not an investment or employment program. Earnings depend on task availability and completion. Results vary per user.

1. Types of Tasks Available on EarnSphere

After logging into your dashboard, you’ll see different categories of tasks. Each task shows the reward amount in Naira before you start. Common task types include:

  • Survey Completion: Answer questions about products, services, or consumer habits. Rewards typically range from ₦50 - ₦300 per survey.
  • App Testing: Download and test new mobile apps. Provide feedback on user experience. Rewards: ₦100 - ₦500.
  • Video Engagement: Watch promotional or educational videos and answer a short question after. Rewards: ₦20 - ₦100 per video.
  • Daily Check-in: Log in daily to claim a small bonus. This helps active users build their balance over time.
  • Referral Tasks: Invite friends using your referral link. You receive a bonus when they sign up and complete their first task.

New tasks are added to the dashboard regularly. Checking your dashboard daily gives you the best chance to claim available rewards.

2. How Points and Balance Work

EarnSphere displays your balance in Naira (₦) directly on your dashboard. There is no complicated points conversion. When you complete a task, the Naira amount is added to your “Wallet Balance” instantly.

Viewing Your Balance

Your current balance is always visible at the top of the dashboard and withdraw page. The balance updates automatically after each completed task. You can track your earning history in the “Transactions” section.

3. Withdrawal Requirements & Process

You can request a withdrawal once your balance meets the minimum threshold. This ensures processing fees are covered and reduces failed transactions.

  • Minimum Withdrawal: ₦1,000
  • Supported Banks: All Nigerian banks including Access, GTBank, First Bank, UBA, Zenith, Kuda, Opay, and Palmpay
  • Processing Time: Withdrawals are reviewed and sent within 24-48 hours on business days
  • Information Needed: Bank name, 10-digit account number, and account name matching your EarnSphere profile

4. How to Request a Withdrawal

Withdrawing your earnings is straightforward:

  1. Go to the Withdraw page from your dashboard
  2. Enter the amount you wish to withdraw (minimum ₦1,000)
  3. Select your bank and enter your account details carefully
  4. Confirm the account name matches your bank records
  5. Click “Request Withdrawal”. You’ll receive an email confirmation

Once submitted, your balance is deducted and the request is sent to our payment team. You’ll receive the funds in your bank account within 48 hours if all details are correct.

5. Tips to Maximize Your Earnings

  • Complete your profile: Users with full profiles get matched with more surveys
  • Enable notifications: Get alerts when high-paying tasks are posted
  • Be consistent: Logging in 3-4 times weekly gives better results than once monthly
  • Answer honestly: Survey tasks require genuine responses. Inconsistent answers may disqualify you

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is EarnSphere free to join?
A: Yes. We never ask for payment to join or withdraw. If anyone asks you to pay, it’s a scam.

Q: Why is my balance different on the withdraw page?
A: Your balance syncs across pages. If it looks different, refresh the page. Both pages read from the same Wallet Balance.

Q: What if my withdrawal fails?
A: Failed withdrawals due to wrong account details are refunded to your EarnSphere balance within 24 hours. You can then resubmit with correct info.
