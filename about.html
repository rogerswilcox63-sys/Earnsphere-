📖 Who We Are

EarnSphere Hub is an open, community-first micro-task space designed to bridge the gap between digital growth platforms and organic user engagement across Nigeria.

Our prototype network empowers students, side-hustlers, and everyday smartphone users to monetize small fragments of their spare time by interacting with verified local digital apps, opinion panels, and basic social media workflows.

🎯 Our Core Commitments

  • Transparent Ledger: No hidden registration tiers or mandatory upgrade paywalls.
  • Accessible Boundaries: Low ₦1,000 threshold cashouts tailored directly for local Nigerian digital banking systems.
  • Data Protection: Absolute isolation of personal data clusters from third-party advertising harvesters.
